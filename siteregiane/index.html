EYEBROW DESIGNER

SHADOW EYEBROWS

Semi-permanent makeup technique used to create fuller, more defined eyebrows. This technique involves the use of a small machine that deposits tiny dots of pigment into the skin, creating a soft, powdery effect that mimics the look of makeup. session can take 2 hours.

FULL FACE WAXING

process of removing unwanted hair from the entire face using wax. This comprehensive service typically includes the removal of hair from the forehead, cheeks, eyebrows, upper lip, chin, and sometimes the sides of the face and jawline. It takes about 30 to 60 minutes.

EYEBROWS THREADING

Technique used to shape and define eyebrows. It involves using a thin, twisted cotton or polyester thread, which is rolled over unwanted hair, plucking the hair at the follicle level. Session takes about 15 to 20 minutes.

THREADING DESIGNER WITH HENNA

Technique it's combination of two beauty techniques: eyebrow threading and henna application. This service is aimed at shaping and defining the eyebrows using threading, followed by enhancing their appearance with henna, a natural dye. Typically 15-20 minutes, depending on the desired intensity of the color.

MICRONEEDLING WITH VITAMIN

Microneedling with vitamin infusion in the esthetic business is a skin treatment that combines the benefits of microneedling with the application of vitamins and other beneficial serums to enhance skin rejuvenation and overall health. This technique aims to improve skin texture, reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, and address various skin concerns like hyperpigmentation and acne scars. ession lasts about 60 to 90 minutes.

BROWS LAMINATE

It's a procedure in the esthetic business designed to enhance the appearance of eyebrows by making them look fuller, more defined, and neatly groomed. Session it is about 30 to 45 minutes.

BROWS LAMINATE WITH COLOR

treatment that combines brow lamination and tinting. This service aims to create fuller, more defined eyebrows by reshaping the brow hairs and enhancing their color. It takes about 45 to 60 minutes.

THREADING FOR LIPS

Hair removal technique that uses a thin, twisted cotton thread to remove unwanted hair from the upper and lower lip areas. This method is popular for its precision and effectiveness in removing fine and coarse hair.Threading does not involve the use of chemicals or heat, making it suitable for sensitive skin and reducing the risk of skin irritation or burns. It can takes about 5 to 10 minutes.

MICRONEEDLING NANO HAIR STROKES

is a cutting-edge technique combining microneedling and advanced nanotechnology to create natural-looking hair strokes, particularly for eyebrows. This method aims to mimic the appearance of individual hairs, offering a semi-permanent solution for those seeking fuller, more defined brows. The session takes about 1.5 to 2 hours.

MIX MICRONEEDLING AND SHADOW

It's a integrating of two distinct cosmetic procedures to enhance skin and eyebrow appearance. Microneedling which is a minimally invasive cosmetic procedure that involves using fine needles to create hundreds of tiny, invisible puncture wounds in the top layer of the skin, and After the skin has healed from microneedling (usually a few days to a week), the shadow brows procedure can be performed. The improved skin texture from microneedling can potentially help the pigment from the shadow brows procedure to adhere better and look more even. 30 to 60 minutes of microneedling session and 2 to 3 hours of shadow brows.
